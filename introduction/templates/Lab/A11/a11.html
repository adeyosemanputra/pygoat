What is Insecure Design

Insecure design is a broad category representing different weaknesses, expressed as “missing or ineffective control design.” A secure design can still have implementation defects, but an insecure design can never be fixed by implementation alone.

This lab demonstrates how insecure business logic can lead to a major flaw. The user can get 5 free tickets for a movie, but must wait until all tickets are sold out. Due to insecure design, this logic can be bypassed.

Mitigation

  • Use a secure SDLC
  • Apply threat modeling
  • Validate business logic
  • Limit resource usage
  • Segregate tenants and tiers
